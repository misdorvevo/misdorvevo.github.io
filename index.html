About Me

Will Hatch is a junior at Berklee College of Music, as well as a hip-hop and electronic music producer. He has released many albums under the name vvill, and has produced countless records with the group Connxtion as well.

Purpose Statement

This project is meant to solve a small but annoying step in the release process for independent artists.

Using my tool, you will be able to create a release plan for your upcoming music releases.

This issue is one that I face, being a bad organizer, and it does not have to take the fun out of releasing music!

This tool will not step on your toes, and is meant to be flexible to what your goals are as an artist.
